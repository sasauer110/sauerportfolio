We had to create a video in a group, to promote something that we like about Millersville University for possible incoming freshman. our group decided to showcase the dorms. We devolped a story board and script to go out and have a plan to make the shooting process quick. it also helped plan out the camera shots to follow our video desgin principals. We used a Sony video camera and a tripod to get all of our shots. The dorm we chose was East village A. From there we went inside and got some shots of the common area, drom rooms, kitchen, laundry room, and th study room. After we shot the video we went into Imovie to begin editing where we ultimatley had to drop the laundry room scene because of time restraints. Finally the videos were uploaded to youtube.
