Student at Millersville University

A bit about me, I am a freshman here at Millersville University. My goal here is to graduate with at degree in Applied engineering general industrial technology. For the future I want to become a technology person with a major league baseball club looking at players metrics. I am currently a student assistant with the baseball team here and once the technology arrives, I will start to already learn how it works and how to read it. In the applied engineering program, I get to learn a whole bunch of technology related items such as materials, electronics, drafting and design.

For AENG 110 communication and information systems, I am making this website for a portfolio of the projects I have completed. This class covers the basics in the design principals and how we use them to communicate ideas while keeping the readers engaged. In this class we created a bookmark for a local elementary school, created a video for possible incoming freshman, an infographic on a communication device, and we did some digital photography. The skills all get tied together here for this last project on the website. Thanks for looking at this website and hope you enjoy what you see!
